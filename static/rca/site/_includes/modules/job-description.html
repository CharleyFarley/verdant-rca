Job Description

The Global Innovation Design (GID) programme is a new programme and offers a two-year double Masters MA (RCA) / MSC (Imperial College London) where students study in London, New York and Tokyo.

The programme is run jointly with Imperial College London and currently collaboratively with Keio university in Japan and the Pratt Institute in the U.S.A. It takes a diverse range of applicants (engineers, technologists, designers, commercially experienced people and from many other disciplines) and introduces them.
